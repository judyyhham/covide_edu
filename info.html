COVID-19 Information



Common Symptoms Include:

fever, body ache, dry cough, fatigue, chills, headache, sore throat, loss of appetite, and loss of smell.

Severe Symptoms:

High fever, severe cough, shortness of breath, which often indicates pneumonia.

People with COVID-19 Experience:

neurological symptoms, gastrointestinal (GI) symptoms, or both. These may occur with or without respiratory symptoms.
Neurological Symptoms include: loss of smell, inability to taste, muscle weakness, tingling or numbness in the hands and feet, dizziness, confusion, delirium, seizures, and stroke.
Gastrointestinal Symptoms include: loss of appetite, nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, and abdominal pain or discomfort

Cautious:

Some people infected with the virus have no symptoms.
